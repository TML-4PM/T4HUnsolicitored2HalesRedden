What is a "Pack"?

A Pack is a recurring intelligence subscription for one SME client. It runs automatically each week — analysing their financial data, generating reports, and emailing insights directly to the client. You configure it once. It runs forever.

📊

Your margin on each pack

You buy at wholesale and set your own retail price. A typical client paying $299/mo = ~$133 margin to you. 100 clients = $13,300/mo new recurring revenue from existing relationships.
