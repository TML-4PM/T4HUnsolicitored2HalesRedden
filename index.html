What is the Neural Ennead?

The Neural Ennead is a 9×9×9 autonomous agent architecture — 9 specialist families, each containing 9 teams of 9 agents = 729 total. It's not a chatbot. It's a structured organisation of AI workers with defined roles, accountability, and execution patterns.
